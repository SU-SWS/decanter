A basic, responsive single column page layout.

Example
Content Header

This is a block of content that might appear just above the content area. In Figma, this is shown as a large image of the Quad, and occupies the entire width of a single column layout until the large breakpoint, at which point it only occupies 10 of the 12 columns.

Main Content

This is the main content area of the page. In Figma, this area occupies the entire width of a single column layout until the medium breakpoint, at which point it occupies 8 of the 12 columns. At the large breakpoint, it begins to occupy 10 of the 12 columns.

Content Footer

This is a block of content that might appear just below the content area. In Figma, it is represented by a wide angle view of the approach to the Quad, and its behavior is the same as that of the header.

A basic, responsive 2-column (with right sidebar) page layout.

Example
Content Header

This is a block of content that might appear just above the content area. In Figma, this is shown as a large image of the Quad, and occupies the entire width of both two-column layouts throughout all breakpoints.

Main Content

This is the main content area of the page. In Figma, this area occupies the entire width of a two column layout until the medium breakpoint, at which point it occupies the first 8 of 12 columns.

Sidebar

This is the only sidebar in a right sidebar layout. In Figma, it is represented by a faculty photo, office hours, and other info. It appears below the main content until the medium breakpoint, at which point it moves to the right of the content, occupying 4 of 12 columns.

Content Footer

This is a block of content that might appear just below the content area. It is not illustrated in this layout in Figma, but its behavior would likely match that of the content header.

4.4 Templates.Page Layout: three-column Page Layout: Three column

Toggle full screen Open in new window Toggle example guides Toggle HTML markup

A basic, responsive three-column page layout.

Example
Content Header

This is a block of content that might appear just above the content area. In Figma, this is shown as a large image of the Quad, and occupies the entire width of both two-column layouts throughout all breakpoints.

Main Content

This is the main content area of the page. In Figma, this area occupies the entire width of a three column layout until the medium breakpoint, at which point it occupies the first 8 of 12 columns. At the large breakpoint, it occupies the middle 6 of 12 columns, sidebars to its right and left.

First Sidebar

In Figma, this sidebar is represented by the section menu system. Because it is a menu, it does not appear in Figma until the large breakpoint, at which point it occupies the first 3 of 12 columns.

Second Sidebar

In Figma, this sidebar is represented by a black & white photo and some text. It appears below the main content until the medium breakpoint, at which point it moves to the right of the content, occupying 4 of 12 columns. At the large breakpoint, it occupies 3 of 12 columns.

Content Footer

This is a block of content that might appear just below the content area. It is illustrated as a set of links in Figma; its behavior matches that of the content header.
